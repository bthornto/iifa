In High Pressure Processing (HPP) our bottles of juices are immersed in cold water and then subjected to enormous pressure to inactivate pathogens.

This process allows us to ensure safety and help retain flavors and nutrients. As part of this process the temperature of the cold juice rises minimally, but not above room temperature, and quickly falls.

All of the batches of juice are painstakingly reviewed for quality.

What if we could connect data from quality assurance to customer satisfaction?

That's what we've done.
